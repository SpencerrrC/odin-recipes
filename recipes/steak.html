Spencer's Steak is the Best!

For this recipe you will need a few things:

  • Steak cut of your choice
  • Unsalted Butter
  • BUTTERRRR!!!!
  • 3 sticks of Rosemary
  • 1 clove of Garlic

Now, on with the recipe

  1. Preheat oven

Set the oven to about 300 degrees Fahrenheit. Though I'm going off memory, so you'll have to forgive me if this is way too high. To test the heat, stick your head in the oven to check it's hot enough.(Note, I am not responsible for any injuries caused by this. Do it at your own risk, and make sure to educate yourself properly before doing anything.)

  1. Steak, IN

Put that meat in the oven and let it sit for like 10-15 minutes. Talk with your friends!

  1. Sear UP!

Take out the pan(with a cloth to not burn you) and set the flame on high. Start searin' and clearin', until there's a nice brown crust on all sides.

  1. Everything Comes Together

Put in your butter, enough so that when it's melted there's at least 1.5 spoonfuls of liquid remaining. Then throw in the garlic and other herbs. Tilt the pan away from you, then scoop the melted butter herb mix onto the top of your steak constantly for about 3-5 minutes.

  1. Enjoy

Now bask in the glory and know that you just saved the barbecue by giving your guests something they actually want to eat. Yay!
